Rules & Guardrails

Core ATS Optimization Directives

Our agentic engine enforces strict design and content policies to ensure your resume scores in the top 1% of candidate filters.

1. Quantification Requirement

The engine flags bullets lacking concrete metrics. Every achievement must be quantified (e.g., efficiency gain, query latency reduced, error margins cut) to demonstrate direct business impact.

2. Zero Action Verb Repetition

Starting multiple experience bullets with the same action verb is prohibited. The AI automatically parses sentence structures and replaces duplicate starter verbs with diverse alternatives.

3. Contextual Keyword Flow

Instead of dense, spammy keyword stuffing, which modern ATS parsers flag, our algorithm distributes required skills organically into experience descriptions and professional summaries.

Session-Local Privacy Policy

Your personal resume profile and job target entries are never permanently written to database storage or sold to external brokers. Data is processed in-memory for the duration of the tailoring cycle and exists purely within your local browser session.

Multi-Model Fallback & Resiliency

Our agentic pipeline is built on a resilient architecture using primary and fallback models (Gemini Flash & Pro versions). If a rate limit or API block occurs, the fallback model seamlessly takes over to ensure reliable, uninterrupted generation.
